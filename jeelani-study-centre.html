JEELANI STUDY CENTRE

Upholding the Sufi message of Holy Hazrat Quthbuzzaman and the esteemed Guru Naeb Quthbuzzaman, Jeelani Study Centre (reg. no. 732/02) functions as the pivotal platform for propagation of peace and spiritual progress, dynamically representing the mission of Quthbuzzaman Jeelani Shareef. Initiated by Hazrat Quthbuzzaman in 2002, Jeelani Study Centre stands as a pious platform for da’wah (propagation of Sufism), serving the society, and fostering educational development and community reform. The Centre operates as a propagational social platform as well as the nation-wide network of Qadiri – Chishti Sufi network guided by Jeelani Shareef, under the enduring guidance and patronage of holy Sufi Guru Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Shaikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti.

Jeelani Study Centre (JSC) conducts cultural campaigns, humanitarian social services, and Sufi-intellectual study sessions; particularly, for the disciples and the seekers from the wider public, in general, on regular basis. Being the active servants of its mission, the diverse array of members of JSC volunteer the regular social and religious gatherings at Quthbuzzaman Jeelani Shareef – Kerala on weekly and monthly intervals. As a rapidly growing organization with extensive social engagement and moral commitment, JSC operates through in approximately a hundred local and regional units and area committees across all districts of Kerala. The organization's influence extends significantly, encompassing numerous working units throughout the nation, with an expanding international footprint notably across the UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and Oman. Essentially, the Study Centre provides a robust organizational platform for the Qadiri Sufi followers to reach out to the wider society, facilitate interfaith dialogues, and conduct Sufi empowerment sessions.

Carrying forward the noble Sufi – social mission of Quthbuzzaman Jeelani Shareef, Jeelani Study Centre is deeply dedicated to promoting ethical conduct in society and peaceful co-existence of communities. The historic ‘No Walls Between the Humans’ - Mega Milad Rally (September 2023) conducted in the hometown of Jeelani Shareef (Aluva, Kerala), led by Holy Sufi Shaikh Naeb Quthbuzzaman, stands as a landmark event, being a testament to the organizational efficiency of the Jeelani movement and its unwavering commitment to the cause of humanity at large. Being the vigorous mobilizers and selfless volunteers, the followers of the Study Centre frequently participate in the humanitarian and charitable mission of Jeelani Trust, led by the Holy Sufi Sheikh Naeb Quthbuzzaman. With a strong presence across all social media platforms, Jeelani Study Centre meticulously works toward fulfilling its foundational motto: mahabbah, khidhma, da’wah (love, service, and propagation). With an increased impetus and wider impact, currently, the Study Centre drives to achieve the reaffirmed objectives of ‘Irada 2025’ – the social and moral empowerment programme launched under ‘Jeelani Project – 2005’.
