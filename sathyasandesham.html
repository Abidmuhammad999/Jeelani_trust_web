Sathyasandesham Monthly

A distinguished Sufi monthly periodical published in Malayalam by Jeelani Trust, ‘Santhyasandesham’ serves as the eloquent voice and ‘message of Truth’ representing Jeelani mission for Sufi social and ethical reform, dedicated to realize peaceful humanity and universal harmony. The printed magazine carries the moral and social ideals and values upheld by the Sufi Guru Hazrat Quthbuzzaman Shaikh Yousuf Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti and his Sufi successor and the patron of Jeelani Shareef – Kerala, Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti.

Featuring essays, contemporary commentary pieces, research articles, translations of classical Sufi writings, Sufi stories and poetry, the Sathyasandesham presents the readers with a plethora of refreshing ideas to inspire positivity and change. The pivotal section of the Sufi monthly is devoted to ‘Kalam’, the transcription of the speeches and sermons given by the holy Sufi Guru Hazrat Quthbuzzaman, and ‘Bab’, the lead message and ethical guidance to the world from the supreme leader of Jeelani Sufi mission, Holy Hazrat Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti.

The magazine also regularly carries the monthly message from the President of the Jeelani Study Centre, Shaikh Ahmad Kabeer Sulthan, and a column in English presented by the chief advisor of Nizamia educational venture, His Holiness Akbar Ali Sulthan. With an active and professional editorial board, the monthly Sufi journal of Jeelani Trust is published under H.H. Akbar Ali Sulthan, guided by the esteemed Chairman of the Trust, Holy Hazrat Naeb Quthbuzzaman.

As a literary publication of the Jeelani movement, published from Quthbuzzaman Jeelani Shareef – Kerala, Sathyasandesham Monthly also carries the news feature capturing the activities in Jeelani Shareef, reports and updates on the campaigns and conferences held under Jeelani Study Centre, and covering the academic programmes and educational seminars at Nizamia Institute. An informational and intellectual cultural magazine, Sathyasandesham stands also as an inspirational Sufi companion for those who seek practical guidance in moral life and positive insights for ethical futures for humanity.
